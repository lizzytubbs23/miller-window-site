Pricing

Getting a fair, accurate quote is simple.

We provide residential and commercial window cleaning pricing throughout the Cedar Valley — Waterloo, Cedar Falls, Waverly, and the surrounding communities. Our team can turn around a fast, accurate estimate no matter the size of the job.

Before you call, it helps to do a quick walk around your home or building and count the windows, noting the type and roughly how many you have. Don't worry about getting it exact — we'll verify the count in person before any work begins.

Send us a few photos, and we can do the rest. The single best way to get a precise quote is a few pictures of each side of the home or building, taken from outside. Photos let us account for window size, style, and accessibility before we ever step on-site — just text or email them once you reach out.

What affects your price:

  • Number of windows
  • Window size and style
  • Accessibility and building height
  • Interior only, exterior only, or both
  • Storm windows or specialty glass
  • One-time visit vs. recurring service

Whether it's a single-story home, a storefront, or a multi-story commercial building, we'll customize a quote around what you actually need — no upselling, no pressure, just honest pricing from a family-owned, insured window cleaning company.

Ready for your estimate? Submit your info and you'll get an Instant Quote by email. Have questions along the way? Just give us a call — we're happy to help.

A few notes on pricing

  • Casement pricing applies to standard 3'x2' windows — larger casements will cost more.
  • Slider windows may cost more if the sash needs to be removed for cleaning.
  • Storm windows with French panes may incur additional charges.
  • A $95 minimum service charge applies for small jobs like just a few hard-to-reach windows.
  • Final pricing also depends on window count, accessibility, building height, interior/exterior needs, and service frequency.
