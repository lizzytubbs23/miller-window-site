Our Story

A family business, since 1994.

Three decades of washing windows across the Cedar Valley — same family, same friendly faces, same streak-free shine.

Miller Window Service is a family run and family-owned window cleaning company that has been providing superior professional window cleaning services to residential and commercial customers since 1994 throughout Waterloo, Cedar Falls, Waverly and surrounding Cedar Valley communities.

As a trusted local window cleaning company, we specialize in residential and commercial services including:

  • Interior window cleaning
  • Exterior window cleaning
  • Storefront window cleaning
  • High window cleaning services
  • Streak-free window cleaning
  • Window washing in Waterloo, IA
  • Window washing in Cedar Falls, IA

Over the years, we have built strong relationships with homeowners and business owners who rely on us for dependable, high-quality service. Whether customers need residential window cleaning Waterloo or commercial window cleaning Cedar Falls, we are proud to deliver results that make properties look their best.

Our Team

The people who show up at your door.

Photo of Seth & Kristi Miller

Seth & Kristi Miller

Seth and Kristi live in the area and have two children who have helped out with window washing over the years. Miller Window Service was started by Seth's father in 1994, and Seth took over in 1996. In the early years, Seth and Kristi cleaned many of the windows themselves — today they remain actively involved in the company and continue to value personal relationships with both customers and employees. Their philosophy is simple: treat people well and provide honest, dependable service.

Photo of Ramiro & Ingrid

Ramiro & Ingrid

Ingrid joined the company in 2015, and since 2020 she and her husband Ramiro have worked together as part of the team. They live in Waterloo and serve many homes and businesses throughout Waverly and the Cedar Valley. If you own a local business, chances are you may see them providing storefront or commercial window cleaning in your area — customers love their friendly personalities and attention to detail.

Photo of Virginia

Virginia

Virginia joined the team at the end of 2023 and helps customers with scheduling and communication. She enjoys getting to know both current and new customers and helping coordinate window cleaning services throughout the area.

Photo of Alex Miller

Alex Miller

Alex is Seth and Kristi's son and pitches in with the family business alongside the rest of the crew, carrying on the same hands-on, customer-first approach that's been part of Miller Window Service since day one.

At Miller Window Service, we truly love our team and hope you will too. If you've been searching online for "window washers near me," we would love the opportunity to earn your trust and show you the difference professional care can make.
