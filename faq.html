Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to what we hear most.

Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out and we'll get back to you the same business day.

Do you do inside windows too?

Yes, we clean both the inside and outside of your windows as part of a standard visit.

What's all included when you wash my windows?

A standard cleaning covers the glass, tracks, and sills, inside and out, so the whole window looks cared for — not just the pane.

Do you clean storm windows?

Yes. Storm windows are priced at $60 each, with an additional charge for storm windows with French panes since they take longer to clean properly. See our pricing page for full details.

Can you clean windows three stories high?

Yes, our crews are equipped to safely handle multi-story homes and buildings up to three stories.

I only have a few hard-to-reach windows — can you still help?

Yes, we can still take care of just a few hard-to-reach windows. Because of the equipment and time involved, there is a $95 minimum service charge on every job.

Do I need to be home for an estimate?

No, you don't need to be home. In most cases we can put together an estimate over the phone or from photos of your home.

Do you offer any discounts?

We may be able to offer discounts depending on how flexible you can be with scheduling, and if you'd like your windows cleaned more than twice a year. Mention this when you request your quote. See our specials page for more.

What if it rains on my scheduled day?

We keep an eye on the forecast and will reach out ahead of time to reschedule if weather won't allow for a quality, streak-free clean.

Do you work through the winter?

Yes, we're out working year-round, including through the winter months, weather permitting.

Are you insured?

Yes — Miller Window Service is liability insured and our crews follow strict safety standards on every job.

Still have questions?

Reach out directly and we'll get you an answer the same business day.
